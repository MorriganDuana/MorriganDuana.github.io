Le VulgaNum

Cybersecurity and Digital News Journal


I am the current founder of this journal. My goal was to vulgarize to as many people as possible some principles of cybersecurity of digital news in general.

In 2021, we are a team of 4 composed of a penetration tester, an AI specialist, a system administrator and a cyber security consultant. We are trying to diversify to reach certain sectors of the IT industry.

An amateur newspaper

An amateur newspaper, yes, but it contains real news topics.It is intended for everyone, whether you are a manager or an operational employee. Of course, we are in popularization and we do not go into the technical details of an attack, we schematize but we always leave the opportunity to the most technical to consult the papers made by the researchers that we publish.
